COLD SPRINGS RESORT & RV PARK

Tucked beneath the towering Ponderosa and Tamarack trees and only steps from the spring-fed Metolius River, the cabins and RV park at Cold Springs Resort offer the perfect retreat.

Hiking, biking and world-class fly-fishing opportunities abound and all are within walking distance. Bring the kids, the dog and let yourself truly unwind.

Historic getaway on the Metolius River

The tumbling Metolius River has a long history of bringing families together for vacation fun and relaxation. Today the river views and smell of pine trees continue to draw all ages to Cold Springs Resort for the simplicity only nature can provide.
The clear and cold water of the Metolius River is the perfect habitat for trout. Be sure to bring your fly fishing gear on your next visit.

FUN FOR EVERYONE

Since children and dogs naturally go well together, your pet is welcome here. Your best friend will find a special basket waiting for them with items they might need while visiting the Cold Springs Resort.
